About Myself

I am a second-year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto pursuing a degree in Finance, Economics, and Computer Science. My interests include software and web devlopment, as well as statistical analysis. One of my personal projects that I am currently working on is an algorithmic stock trading program written in Python.

I am especially passionate about working with big data, where I am able to utilize my analytical skills to not only create novel solutions, but to also leave a tangible impact in industries. More specifically, I wish to explore the shrinking gap between business and technology, which is now smaller than ever. On campus, I serve as a finance manager in the Rotman Commerce Entrepreneurship Organization.
